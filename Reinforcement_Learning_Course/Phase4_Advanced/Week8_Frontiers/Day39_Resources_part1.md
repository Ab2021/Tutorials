# Day 39 Deep Dive: Staying Current in RL Research

## 1. Where to Find Papers
*   **arXiv.org:** Latest preprints (check daily: cs.LG, cs.AI).
*   **Conferences:** NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CoRL, IROS, RSS.
*   **Journals:** JMLR, IEEE T-RO, Science Robotics.

## 2. Following Researchers
Key figures to follow on Twitter/X, Google Scholar:
*   Sergey Levine (Berkeley)
*   Pieter Abbeel (Berkeley, Covariant AI)
*   Chelsea Finn (Stanford)
*   David Silver (DeepMind)
*   Richard Sutton (Alberta, DeepMind)

## 3. Blogs & Newsletters
*   **OpenAI Blog:** Latest research and deployments.
*   **DeepMind Blog:** AlphaGo, MuZero, etc.
*   **Lil'Log (Lilian Weng):** Outstanding RL summaries.
*   **The Batch (Andrew Ng):** Weekly AI news.
*   **Import AI (Jack Clark):** AI policy and research.

## 4. Communities
*   **Reddit:** r/reinforcementlearning, r/MachineLearning.
*   **Discord:** Various RL servers (RL Discord, ML Collective).
*   **Twitter/X:** Follow #ReinforcementLearning hashtag.

## 5. Reproduce Papers
*   Best way to learn: Implement papers from scratch.
*   Start with classics (DQN, PPO), move to recent work.
*   Compare your results to reported benchmarks.
