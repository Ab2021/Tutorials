# Day 59: Interview Questions & Answers

## Behavioral Questions

### Q1: Tell me about a time you disagreed with a coworker.
**Answer:**
*   **Focus**: Professionalism and Data.
*   **Story**: "Coworker wanted MongoDB. I wanted Postgres. I benchmarked both for our use case. Postgres won. We went with Postgres. No hard feelings."

### Q2: Tell me about a challenging bug.
**Answer:**
*   **Focus**: Persistence and Debugging Skills.
*   **Story**: "Race condition in payment processing. Hard to reproduce. I added detailed logging, reproduced it in staging, used a mutex to fix it."

### Q3: Why do you want to work here?
**Answer:**
*   **Focus**: Research.
*   **Story**: "I love your product X. I saw your engineering blog post about Y. I want to work on Z challenges."

## Role-Specific Questions

### Q4: How do you handle tight deadlines?
**Answer:**
*   **Prioritize**: "I cut scope. MVP first."
*   **Communicate**: "I tell stakeholders early if we are slipping."

### Q5: What is your ideal work environment?
**Answer:**
*   **Honesty**: "Collaborative, Code Reviews, CI/CD, Psychological Safety."

---

## Red Flags (Avoid these)
*   "My boss was an idiot." (Never badmouth).
*   "I just want money." (Even if true, don't say it).
*   "I work alone." (Software is a team sport).
