## 1. Course Recap: The Journey

We have covered the entire Deep Learning stack:

You are no longer a beginner. You have the theoretical depth and practical skills of a Senior MLE.

## 2. The Modern ML Stack

*   **Framework**: PyTorch (Research & Production).
*   **Training**: Hugging Face Trainer, Accelerate, Lightning.
*   **Inference**: TensorRT, vLLM, TGI.
*   **Deployment**: Docker, Kubernetes, Ray Serve.
*   **Ops**: MLflow (Tracking), WandB (Viz), Kubeflow (Pipelines).

## 3. Career Paths

1.  **Research Scientist**:
    *   Focus: New algorithms, math, publishing papers.
    *   Skills: Math, LaTeX, Novelty.
    *   Goal: Push the SOTA.
2.  **Machine Learning Engineer (MLE)**:
    *   Focus: Training large models, scaling, productionizing.
    *   Skills: Distributed Systems, CUDA, Python, SQL.
    *   Goal: Build working systems.
3.  **Applied Scientist**:
    *   Hybrid. Applies SOTA to business problems.
    *   Focus: Fine-tuning, RAG, Domain adaptation.

## 4. Future Trends (2025+)

*   **Agents**: LLMs that take action (browse web, use tools).
*   **On-Device AI**: SLMs (Small Language Models) running on phones.
*   **Video Generation**: Sora, Runway. Physics simulation.
*   **Reasoning**: System 2 thinking (Chain of Thought, Tree of Thoughts).

## 5. Final Words

Deep Learning is an experimental science.
*   **Read Papers**: ArXiv Sanity.
*   **Read Code**: Hugging Face, Timm, PyTorch source.
*   **Build**: Clone repos and break them.
