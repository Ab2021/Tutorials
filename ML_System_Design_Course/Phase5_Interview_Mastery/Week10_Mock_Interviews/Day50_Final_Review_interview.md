# Day 50: Final Review & Career - Interview Questions

> **Topic**: Career Growth & Negotiation
> **Focus**: 20 Popular Interview Questions with Detailed Answers

### 1. How do you negotiate your salary?
**Answer:**
*   **Leverage**: Have multiple offers.
*   **Market Data**: Know your worth (Levels.fyi).
*   **Focus**: Negotiate Total Comp (Equity + Bonus), not just Base.

### 2. What questions should you ask the hiring manager?
**Answer:**
*   "How is the team structured?"
*   "What is the on-call rotation like?"
*   "How are decisions made?"

### 3. How do you choose between a Startup and Big Tech?
**Answer:**
*   **Startup**: High impact, broad scope, high risk, equity lottery.
*   **Big Tech**: Scale, mentorship, stability, liquid comp.

### 4. What is "Equity" (RSU vs Options)?
**Answer:**
*   **RSU**: Actual shares. Value = Price. Liquid in public co.
*   **Options**: Right to buy at Strike Price. Value = Price - Strike. Risky.

### 5. How do you handle "Exploding Offers"?
**Answer:**
*   Ask for extension.
*   "I need time to finish my other interviews to make an informed decision."
*   Most deadlines are soft.

### 6. What is "Leveling"?
**Answer:**
*   L3 (Junior), L4 (Mid), L5 (Senior), L6 (Staff).
*   Interview performance determines level.
*   Negotiate level, not just money.

### 7. How do you prepare for "On-site" loop?
**Answer:**
*   Stamina (4-5 hours).
*   Mock interviews.
*   Rest well.

### 8. What is a "Staff Engineer"?
**Answer:**
*   Technical Leader.
*   Sets technical direction.
*   Mentors others.
*   Solves cross-team problems.

### 9. How do you handle "Rejection"?
**Answer:**
*   It's a numbers game.
*   Ask for feedback (rarely given).
*   Move on.

### 10. What is "Vestibule" / "Vesting Schedule"?
**Answer:**
*   **Cliff**: 1 year wait before getting anything.
*   **Schedule**: Usually 4 years (25% per year).

### 11. How do you evaluate a company's culture?
**Answer:**
*   Talk to engineers, not just managers.
*   Ask about work-life balance, attrition.

### 12. What is "Impact" in performance review?
**Answer:**
*   Measurable results (Revenue, Latency, Efficiency).
*   "Launched X" is activity. "Improved Y by Z%" is impact.

### 13. How do you handle "Imposter Syndrome"?
**Answer:**
*   Common in tech.
*   Focus on learning.
*   Document your wins.

### 14. What is "Networking"?
**Answer:**
*   Building relationships before you need them.
*   Referrals are the best way to get interviews.

### 15. How do you transition from IC (Individual Contributor) to Manager?
**Answer:**
*   Start by mentoring / leading projects.
*   Express interest to manager.
*   It's a career change, not a promotion.

### 16. What is "Total Compensation" (TC)?
**Answer:**
*   Base + Bonus + Equity (per year) + Sign-on.

### 17. How do you read a "Term Sheet" (Startup)?
**Answer:**
*   Valuation, Preferred Shares, Liquidation Preference.

### 18. What is "Refreshers"?
**Answer:**
*   Additional equity grants given annually to retain employees.

### 19. How do you leave a job gracefully?
**Answer:**
*   Give notice (2 weeks).
*   Document everything.
*   Don't burn bridges.

### 20. What is the most important skill for an ML Engineer?
**Answer:**
*   **Adaptability**. The field changes every 6 months.
