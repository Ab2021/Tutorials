## 4. Future Trends (2025 and Beyond)

### 4.1 Embedded Insurance

*   **Trend:** Insurance is not sold; it is *bought* with the product.
*   **Example:** Buying a Tesla includes insurance. Buying a flight includes travel cover.
*   **Impact:** Distribution costs drop to near zero. API reliability becomes critical.

### 4.2 Parametric Insurance

*   **Trend:** "If Event X happens, Pay \$Y." No claims adjustment.
*   **Example:** If wind speed > 100mph, pay the hotel \$50k.
*   **Impact:** Instant payouts. Blockchain (Smart Contracts) becomes relevant.

### 4.3 GenAI in Underwriting

*   **Trend:** LLMs reading medical reports and engineering diagrams.
*   **Example:** "Summarize this 50-page MRI report and flag high-risk conditions."
*   **Impact:** Underwriters become "Reviewers" rather than "Readers".

## 5. The Ethical Imperative

### 5.1 Algorithmic Fairness

*   **Rule:** You cannot discriminate based on Race, Gender, or Religion.
*   **Challenge:** Proxy variables (Zip Code correlates with Race).
*   **Responsibility:** You must test your models for **Disparate Impact**.

### 5.2 The "Black Box" Regulation

*   **Rule:** You must explain *why* you denied a claim.
*   **Tool:** SHAP / LIME.
*   **Mindset:** "If I can't explain it, I won't deploy it."
